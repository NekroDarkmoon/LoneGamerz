Rules and Regulations

As a member of this clan, you are to:

  1. Show utmost respect towards all others at any point in time
    We, as members of the internet, should observe this simple rule at all times. Not only your clan-mates, or seniors, but any fellow human being in Warframe. If you suspect someone is an alien, respect him anyway.
  2. Refrain from spamming
    You are spamming if you send multiple messages extremely quickly and are met with obvious displeasure, usually in the form of annoyance. Otherwise, you may be sending clone messages without any proper purpose. Failure to halt, even if it was not ill-intended, will be considered spamming. This will be dealt on a case by case basis, and we encourage you to report any such offense (see below).
  3. Refrain from malicious trolling
    Well-meant jokes are shared all the time, but when you fail to stop despite the annoyance of the trolled, you will be considered guilty. Trolls that rely on unwitting or unwillingly unfair trades are one step too far. People love their money.
  4. Never rage
    All of us will have to blow off some steam at some time in our lives. Warframe is not the right time. In Warframe, we aim to all have fun. Don't rage at another person's expense. To this end, an angry tone will be tolerated, but rage of density more than your idiocy will not. Lets make Warframe a place where we can game in peace.
  5. WIKI is love WIKI is life
    If you're new to the game do read the wiki and not ask about every single detail you come across. We love helping others out but come on you cant expect us to explain how going into a mission works <3 .
  6. Refrain from sending unappreciated invites
    No one likes to be spammed with invites when they clearly dont want to accept them. So do yourself a favour and ask before sending out invires to clan members. Violation of this rule will result in chat ban.
  7. NOT say every fucking sentence with fuck put at every fucking 4-word interval like its the fucking end of the fucking world. That's an example. Don't follow it.
  8. DO NOT take everything to heart. We all crack jokes, some might be hilarious some might be offensive. But the key word here is JOKE, its all a joke there is no harm intended within the minds of these people. But if you're still taking offence at these silly jokes just send any overseer a PM and they'll be happy to give you a 24 hour chat ban so you dont have to go through said torture. Love from all.
  9. Speak English
    This is less of a rule and more of an encouragement. Failure to comply results in YOUR DEA- A pm from one of us with a smiley in it along with a cute 1 hour ban from chat. Enjoy your silence.
  10. Anyone offline for 30 days or more will be kicked.
    To get extra time, simply send an email or message on discord/ingame/forums to any of the Overseers or Elders and if you were kicked, sending a message will re-admit you back into the clan.
  11. You will worship the Senpais.
    Actually, don't.

Ranks and Ranking up criteria

  • Overseer Of All - Rank bestowed depending at the discretion of Nekro_Darkmoon.
  • Elder - Rank bestowed upon to those that have been in the clan for 2+ years and upon discretion.
  • Commander - Rank bestowed to those that have been in the clan for 1+ years and lend a helping hand.
  • General - Rank bestowed to those willing to help others.
  • Contributor - Rank bestowed to those willing to donate to dojo.
  • Socializer - Rank bestowed upon conversing in chat.
  • Member - Basic rank bestowed upon joining.

Roles

  • Socializer- Talkkkkkkk!!!!
  • Contributor- On arrival of new research in dojo all contributors are required to help in completing clan research.
  • General- Should help new players or low MR in accomplishing/completing missions or answer their questions.
  • Commander- Role is given to those who carry the responsibilities of the general as well as intoducing new players to participate in quests(specifically clan orianted so we can climb the leader board as a clan) lunaro/conclave/raids/events, etc.
  • Elder- Role will be given to those who have been in a clan for a certain period of time and have done all of the previous tasks alloted by lower ranks.
